What We're About

EHR Match will be an application that will visualize clusterings of patients using electronic health record data. The interactable side of the application will be easy to use, concise, provide patient search capabilities, and make use of graphs and plots to be visually appealing. The graphs and plots mentioned will have customizable variables to tailor the information presented to the needs of the user. The non-interactable side of the application will pull data from a database, process said data, and generate clusters of similar patients given variables that users will provide. In addition to clusters, patient profiles containing demographic information, admittance reasons, and lab vitals will be created for further analysis by the users.
